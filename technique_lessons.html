Mindfulness Techniques

Breath Awareness

Focus on your breath. Notice the sensation as you inhale and exhale.


Body Scan

Scan your body from head to toe, noticing any areas of tension or discomfort.


Mindful Walking

Take a walk outdoors and pay attention to each step, the sights, sounds, and smells around you.


Mindful Eating

Eat slowly and savor each bite, paying attention to the taste, texture, and sensation of the food.


Yoga Poses

Mountain Pose (Tadasana)

Stand tall with feet hip-width apart, arms at sides. Lengthen spine and reach crown of head toward sky.

Downward-Facing Dog (Adho Mukha Svanasana)

Start on hands and knees, lift hips toward ceiling to form inverted V shape. Press hands into ground.

Warrior II (Virabhadrasana II)

Stand with feet wide apart, rotate back foot outward, bend front knee to 90 degrees. Extend arms parallel to ground.

Child's Pose (Balasana)

Kneel on floor, sit back on heels, fold forward, resting forehead on ground. Extend arms in front or alongside body.
